It’s all about input and organize. 

But, what if you could just focus on input. 

And inputing would feel frictionless. The only friction would be your mind and willingness to input. Not the input itself. 

I don’t wanna have to think about what tags I’m going to add to it or how I’m going to organize it. That’s not a job I want to do. I just want to input to remember. 

The magic lies in the app helping me make sense of my inputs and organizing it in a way that is useful. 

Gone are the days of adding tags.
