I can hear spring.

The saying goes “spring is in the air” but here I sit at my desk with the window opened to my left hearing spring.

I can hear the wind hitting chimes.
I can hear the wind hitting gutters.
I can hear the wind hitting loose doors.

This is all possible because my window is open and there is wind. Truly spring is in the air. The air that is flowing. The flow is the wind I hear.

Spring is my favorite season for this reason.
