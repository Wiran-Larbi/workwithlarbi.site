As 2022 comes to a close I've witnessed many rebrands. Whether that be a completely new name or an iteration, I love the process that a rebrand goes through. And what I feel is my favorite is purely subjective. This will be the first time I've shared my yearly opinion. I want to do this every year to showcase my overall stance that also puts a mark on the year.

This year felt like a refinement year—take what was good and reMARKable and refine it, make it simpler. 

With refinement as my overall theme and feel for 2022 [my favorite rebrand was Sprite](https://www.underconsideration.com/brandnew/archives/new_logo_identity_and_packaging_for_sprite_by_turner_duckworth_relative_and_in_house.php).
