## Overview
**SecOraPrep Affiliate** is the dedicated management engine powering the referral ecosystem for the SecOraPrep cybersecurity platform. Built to handle high-volume partner traffic, it provides a seamless bridge between security professionals and the enterprise training services they recommend.

## Core Features
- **Next.js 15 & React 19**: Utilizing the modern App Router for lightning-fast performance and SEO-optimized affiliate portals.
- **Automated Payout Engine**: Logic-driven commission processing with support for multiple payout methods and scheduled cycles (NET-15/30).
- **Advanced Tracking**: Real-time conversion tracking and lead management, allowing affiliates to monitor their impact with granular precision.
- **White-Label Design**: A customized interface that aligns perfectly with the SecOraPrep brand identity, providing a consistent user experience.

## Technical Implementation
The system leverages **Prisma ORM** and **PostgreSQL** for reliable data persistence, managing the intricate relationships between leads, approved conversions, and historical payouts. Authentication and security are prioritized using **JWT** and industry-standard encryption to protect partner earnings data.

## Business Impact
By automating the administrative overhead of partner management, the platform allows SecOraPrep to scale its reach exponentially. It empowers a community of cybersecurity experts to monetize their influence while ensuring a high-quality, professional onboarding experience for new students.
