After spending a couple years designing the Harvous Bible notes app it was time for a proper, simple marketing website. I wanted the layout to be tight and modular—to mimic the design language of the app. This would be the first impressions of my first ever app. 

I took many notes from that of a 37signals website like starting off with a letter. All-in-all I was able to put this together over a weekend mainly because of the [Mast framework](https://www.nocodesupply.co/mast/docs). If you'd like to see it, [it's live here.](https://harvous.com)
